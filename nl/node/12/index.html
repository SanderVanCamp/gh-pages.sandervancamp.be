Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At sandervancamp.be, I believe in a private, tracking-free web experience. This policy explains how this website handles information (or rather, how it doesn't).

1. Data Collection & Cookies

This website is built using Drupal, but it has been configured to be as privacy-respecting as possible:

No Cookies: This site does not set any tracking, advertising, or functional cookies on your browser.

No Sessions: We do not use session identifiers to track your movement across the site.

No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) through this website.

2. Server Logs

Like almost all web servers, the hosting provider may automatically log certain technical information when you visit, such as:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • The date and time of access
  • The page(s) visited

This data is used exclusively for technical maintenance, security monitoring, and ensuring the stability of the website. This information is not used to identify individuals or create user profiles.

3. Third-Party Services

This website does not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), social media plugins, or external tracking scripts that would allow third parties to collect your data.

4. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data held about you. However, since sandervancamp.be does not collect or store personal data, there is typically no data for us to provide or delete.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can reach out via the contact methods provided on the site.
