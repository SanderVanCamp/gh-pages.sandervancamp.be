About Me

I’m Sander Van Camp. I’ve spent most of my career in the PHP and Symfony ecosystem, usually building complex systems with Drupal. I’m not just there to push code—I’m there to make sure the technical side of a project actually serves the people using it.

While Drupal is my go-to for large-scale systems, I’m equally comfortable delivering custom Symfony backends or lighter, effective WordPress sites. On the frontend, I use tools like Tailwind, Alpine.js, or React to keep things fast and interactive. My focus is always the same: building maintainable systems that don’t break when you scale. I’m a fan of the right tool for the job—not just the trendiest one.
