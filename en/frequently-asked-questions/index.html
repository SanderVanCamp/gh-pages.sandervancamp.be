Frequently Asked Questions

How is my application’s health monitored?

I integrate GlitchTip for real-time error tracking and uptime monitoring. This allows me to receive instant notifications for PHP exceptions or 500-level errors, often enabling me to resolve bottlenecks before they impact your end-users.

What happens to my project if you are unavailable?

While I operate as an independent specialist, I maintain an "Emergency Handover" protocol for long-term partners. This includes comprehensive documentation and a network of trusted senior Drupal colleagues who can provide coverage for critical security patches during my absence.

How do you guarantee code quality?

I adhere strictly to Drupal Coding Standards and utilize PHPStan for static analysis. By focusing on "The Drupal Way," I ensure that all custom modules I develop are maintainable by any senior developer and remain compatible with future core updates.

What is your hourly rate and how do you bill?

My standard rate is €65 per hour (excl. VAT). Depending on the nature of your project, I offer two transparent billing models:

Fixed Project Price: For clearly defined scopes with a set beginning and end, I provide a comprehensive quote in advance. This gives you budget certainty for specific milestones or new builds.

Hourly Billing: For ongoing maintenance, architectural consulting, or evolving development needs, I bill by the hour. I provide detailed time-tracking reports with every invoice so you have full visibility into the work performed.

Do you provide web hosting for the projects you build?

While hosting is not my primary business, I can provide managed hosting environments if they fit the technical requirements of the project. I typically architect solutions on Drupal-optimized platforms like Platform.sh or Pantheon, ensuring your site has the high-performance infrastructure it deserves.

Can you set up my professional business emails?

I can facilitate the technical DNS configuration for your mail servers; however, I strongly recommend that clients manage their primary email suite through a dedicated provider like Google Workspace or Microsoft 365. This ensures you have enterprise-grade security, superior uptime, and independent control over your company's communication identity.
